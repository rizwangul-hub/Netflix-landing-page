Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?
+

Netflix is a global subscription-based streaming service offering a vast library of TV shows, movies, anime, and documentaries, including original content, for a monthly fee. It operates on-demand, allowing users to watch content instantly on internet-connected devices like TVs, smartphones, laptops, and game consoles.

How much does netflix cost?
+

Netflix in the US offers three main monthly plans as of early 2026: Standard with ads ($7.99), Standard ($17.99), and Premium ($24.99). These plans differ in ad presence, streaming quality (Full HD to 4K), and the number of devices allowed for simultaneous viewing, ranging from 2 to 4 screens.

How do I cancel?
+

To cancel your Netflix subscription, sign in to your account on a web browser, select your profile, go to the "Account" page, and click "Cancel Membership" under the membership section, followed by "Finish Cancellation". You can continue to stream until the end of your current billing period.

What can I watch on Netflix?
+

As of February 2026, Netflix's trending and top-rated library includes a mix of long-running global hits and new releases. Is netflix is good for kids?
+

Netflix is generally good for kids, provided parents utilize built-in parental controls to manage content and screen time. It offers a dedicated "Kids" profile with age-appropriate, curated content and allows for maturity rating restrictions, PIN protection for profiles, and viewing history monitoring. However, without supervision, it is easy for children to access inappropriate, mature content.
